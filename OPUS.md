## Part II — The Assimilation Rulings (only what is beneficial)

Nine rulings. Each is doc-level canon — declared once, propagated by the normal spec discipline.
None renames a file, breaks a URL, or edits frozen code.

- **R1 · pulse is one word.** Bond Pulse, twin.pulse frames, and the body pulse are one concept
  at three scales (observe → reconcile → append). The LEXICON states it; each doc keeps its
  scale-specific detail. *Why:* today four "pulses" require four explanations; after, one.
- **R2 · organism = being; twin = person-subject organism.** Synonym ruling only; no text
  ripped up. *Why:* "being/organism/twin" ambiguity is the single biggest onboarding fog.
- **R3 · egg and frame are the two faces of one moment.** Frame = published (bones), egg =
  carried (whole). *Why:* it welds the portability story to the biography story with zero new
  machinery, and makes the membrane teach itself.
- **R4 · the brainstem is one organ with three bodies** — local / browser / embedded. The
  headless SDK's filename (`vbrainstem_sdk.py`) is historical and stays (Article V: URL shapes
  are sacred); the LEXICON names it the *embedded body*. RAR/twin HTML copies get MIRROR headers
  + sync (per the coherence proposal). *Why:* resolves the vbrainstem name-collision found today
  without breaking one link.
- **R5 · swarm = many acting as one.** Tier-2 swarm (many functions, one brainstem), neuron
  swarm (many specialists, one question), race (many selves, one problem) — instances of one
  word. Directory names untouched. *Why:* three uses, one definition, zero renames.
- **R6 · the estate collision is named, not resolved by force.** rapp-estate = an operator's
  LAND (their repos); the Wrapped-Organism's five estates = a being's ORGANS. Both entrenched;
  the spine's collisions table gets the relationship (exactly how the Leviathan collision was
  handled — that pattern worked). *Why:* naming a collision kills its confusion at zero cost.
- **R7 · doorman = posting** (ruled earlier today, folded into canon): one primitive, two doors
  (machine-door, persona-door); Heimdall is the reference posting; "front door" repos are doors
  whose doorman happens to be static. *Why:* already evidenced by the H3 drift.
- **R8 · bonds.json and frames are the same events on two sides of the membrane.** bonds.json =
  the GOD-side ledger (private, kernel-local); frames = the DOG-side biography (public). No code
  merge — the relationship is declared, and future frame-emitters MAY mirror bond events as
  frames. *Why:* two append-only records per organism currently look like a contradiction; they
  are a membrane pair.
- **R9 · the composition operator is canon.** `derive + overlay` is written into the LEXICON as
  the organism's one composition move, with its three sightings named. *Why:* it is the deepest
  simplification found today — one operation replacing three explanations.

**Carrier of the rulings:** one new canon file — **`LEXICON.md`** at the species root
(kody-w/RAPP), human-first, one page per the Nine Words + the rulings + the three shelves.
The Constitution gains one append-only article pointing at it. The spine narrative references it
(no new spec id — CRAWL rule 6: this names relationships, it does not invent a protocol). The
ecosystem-spec gains a `lexicon` pointer + version bump, propagated byte-identically to both
mirrors, Bible re-pinned — the exact discipline proven earlier today.
